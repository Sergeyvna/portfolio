Working at the kiosk

Main mechanic of this game is serving the customers with their desired order, in case of a disagreement the player has to interact with unsatisfied customer by entering the text with keyboard.
Each word is then checked with a csv file that contains words with their numerical valence which is used to calculate the overall damage to win in an argument.

Game Engine: Unity
Platform: PC [Windows]
Character design: pikisuperstar [Pixaleted and animated by me]
